The World of Torinth

Torinth is a fantasy realm, the location of TTRPG adventures I run. It is a high fantasy world, where magic has been studied and utilized in common magical devices that resemble many types of real world or sci-fi technologies, from radios to robots. There are both quant villages and immense towering cities. Megacorporations make shady deals while noble paladins hunt dragons. All sorts of adventures can be had in Torinth.
