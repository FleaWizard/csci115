Adventures of the Clouds(l)inger

Characters

  • Poxxy Blue Blaggard, Idden Ranger
  • Oriette Greencleaver, Genasi Barbarian
  • Lily LuLuLemon, Aasimar Cleric
  • Dorlin, Human Warlock
  • Jeeba Cobbleriver, Gnome Artificer

Summary

The party met in an underground fighting ring ran By Corwill, where they fought alchemically altered beasts provided by Distilled Dynamics. After the fight they were kidnapped by Distilled dynamics, but succesfully escaped. They then follewed a lead to Drusall, where a shipment of the substance that mutated the beast was being sent.

In Drusall, they investigated Queen Devere and found out it was her who ordered the substance. However, she offered them a lucrative deal to go after Distilled Dynamics, but leave her alone. The party accepted. She told the party that the substance's main ingredient came from Ascana, and the only way to get there is through the Gate in Alisport

The party arived in Alisport, and found out that the gate was not yet open for public travel. However, they spoke with the head of research, Doctor Fallo, and he agreed to let them use it in exchange for rescuing a grad student from hell.

NPCs

Port Sintague

Corwill: Human male gang leader in Port Sintague. Runs an underground fighting ring out of old abandoned refineries and warehouses under the main city. Seems to have some sort of deal with Distilled Dynamics.

Mrs. Moravi: Affluent resident of Port Sintague. You surveyed her home as a dying child, invited her son to a religious rave, drugged his bodyguard into a pigeon, and used that opportunity to steal her skyyacht.

Vespa

Queen Devere: Vespan Queen of Drusall, a nation created when she forcibly took the territory from the nation of Wrosa. Known as a harsh ruler and adept stategist. Disliked by many Queens for her violent takeover of Drusall, violating the Vespan standards of war. Resentful towards her sister Rille for being made Queen of Wrosa instead of her. Seems to be making illicit purchases from Distilled Dynamics.

Queen Rille: Vespan Queen of Wrosa, one of the largest nations in Torinth. Chosen by her mother to succeed her as queen of Wrosa. Known to be a skilled negotiator and a pacifist. Well regarded by many other Queens.

Alisport

Derrien Maille: Human male Loan shark who has lent money to Jeeba and Queen Devere. Has an office in mid-Alisport penthouse. Known for his flashy garb, perpetual perfect white smile, and cold brutality.

Doctor Fallo: Goblin male Professor of Arcane Physics at Alis Academy of Advancement of the Arcane Arts, and head of research of the Ascana Gate project. Has a complex eyeglass array to compensate for damage vision in a teleportation incident. Very excitable and enthusiastic in his work.
